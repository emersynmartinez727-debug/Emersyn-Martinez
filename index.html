FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about working together and how I can help your book achieve the success it deserves.

What types of books do you work with?

I work with authors across all fiction and non fiction genres. Different genres require different strategies, and during our initial consultation, we will discuss whether your book and goals align perfectly with my expertise and available services.

Do you work with self published and traditionally published authors?

Yes absolutely! I work with authors across all publishing paths including self published, indie, small press, and traditional publishing houses. The marketing strategies differ slightly based on your publishing situation, but the core fundamentals remain the same.

How long until I start seeing results?

Results vary based on your book, current market position, and chosen strategy. Generally, launch campaigns show results within the first 30 days, backlist revivals typically show momentum in 2 to 4 weeks, and series optimization campaigns show improvement in 4 to 8 weeks. I provide realistic timelines during our strategy session.

Why is everything documented via email?

Email documentation provides a clear record of all work completed, complete transparency about budget and results, accountability for both of us, future reference for what worked best, and protection against any misunderstandings. You will have a complete paper trail of our entire project together.

Can you guarantee specific sales numbers or rankings?

No ethical marketer can guarantee specific rankings or sales numbers because we do not control Amazon algorithms, reader behavior, or overall market conditions. What I can guarantee is strategic expertise, professional execution, transparent reporting, and my absolute best effort to achieve your goals.

What if my book has been out for several years already?

That is actually perfect! Many of my most successful campaigns have been backlist revivals. A published book with existing reviews and real performance data is actually easier to market effectively than a brand new release because we have real information to work with and optimize.

How do I get started working with you?

Simply send me an email at emersynmartinez727@gmail.com or fill out the contact form on this page. Include details about your book, your current situation, and your goals. I will review your information and respond within 24 to 48 hours with my initial thoughts and recommendations.
